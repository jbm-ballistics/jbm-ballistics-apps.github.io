Dry Fire

See on the AppStore

The JBM Dry Fire application implements the Steve Anderson dry fire drills as documented in his book Refinement and Repetition: Dry-fire Drills for Dramatic Improvement available through Amazon.com. The app makes it possible to perform dry fire drills with a single tap of a button greatly reducing time wasted in dry firing with a timer.

This application is designed to be a companion app to Steve's book, not a replacement. Please refer to the book for drill documentation.
